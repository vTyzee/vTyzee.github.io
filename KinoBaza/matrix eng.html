Year: 1999
Directors: Lana Wachowski, Lilly Wachowski

Description

Thomas Anderson's life is divided into two parts: during the day, he is an ordinary office worker, reprimanded by his boss, and at night he becomes a hacker named Neo, with no place in the network he can't penetrate. But one day, everything changes. Thomas learns the horrifying truth about reality.
